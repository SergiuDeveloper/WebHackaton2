Ontology Creation

The ontology was modeled using the Protege V5.5.0 ontology editor and the Graph visualisation was generated using OWLGrEd Version 1.6.11.

Scenario & Knowledge Model Description

The use case/scenario considered when modelling the ontology revolves around the idea of Santa distributing appropriate items (toys) to the considered actors (children) while taking into consideration their interests (e.g. Jonny that likes aerospace engineering would likely be interested in an airplane toy) and age (e.g. a 5 year old child with the same interests as Jonny would not like an aircraft model kit witch is recomemeded for ages 10 and up). Santa could also opt to change his delivety method thaking into consideration the weather conditions of different countryes (e.g. Australia is sunny, on average while Russia is Snowy) and the favourable and unfavourable weather conditions for the different delivery methods (e.g. Santa's magic sled might be difficult to drive in a snowstorm, while a snowmobile could be more advantagious).

Based on the ontology, one could use relationship-oriented computational methods in order to achieve the aforementioned task, such as classical inference algorithms, machine learning algorithms (e.g. graph clustering algorithms), or modern deep learning methods (e.g. graph neural nets).
